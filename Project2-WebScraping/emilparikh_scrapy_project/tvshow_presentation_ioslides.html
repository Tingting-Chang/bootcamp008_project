Conclusion

We did see that the number of comedies, reality shows, and dramas are increasing more than other shows. The number of comedies made per year have the most consistent rise. The next steepest rise is in reality shows.

I know I cannot necessarily make assumptions about audiences based on which shows are created, but it seems at least the creators and networks feed us more shows that are "lighter on the brain."

Conclusion

Next steps

TV show data is not enough to answer "where is society headed?", especially without viewership data. Some future steps I would take to build upon this project are:

  • Scrape more lists of TV shows
  • Obtain numbers on the audience side, such as viewership of shows/genres/neworks
  • Compare various data (like genre, viewership) of traditional networks with streaming services such as Netflix
  • Include movies, music, books, magazines, news, etc to the analysis
  • Expand beyond entertainment. Include the trend in degrees and jobs.
